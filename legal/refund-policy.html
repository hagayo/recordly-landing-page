Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Subscriptions

Recordly offers two plans:

  • Free plan: up to 5 recordings per month
  • Pro plan: unlimited recordings

Billing

Pro subscriptions are billed monthly at $5 per month or annually at $45 per year.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Your Pro plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for unused time in monthly plans.

Annual Refunds

If you purchased an annual plan, you may request a refund. The refund is reduced according to the time already used.

The refund will be calculated as:

Refund = $45 - (months used / 12 * $45)

Example: if you used 3 months, the used value is 3 / 12 * $45 = $11.25, so the refund is $33.75.

Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refunds in cases of abuse, fraud, chargeback misuse, or excessive usage inconsistent with normal product use.
