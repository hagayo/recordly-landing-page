Refund Policy

Subscriptions

Recordly offers two plans:

  • Free plan: up to 5 recordings per month
  • Pro plan: unlimited recordings

Billing

Pro subscriptions are billed monthly ($5) or annually ($45).

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription at any time. Your plan will remain active until the end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are issued for unused time in monthly plans.

Annual Refunds

If you purchased an annual plan, you may request a refund. The refund will be calculated as:

Refund = $45 − (months used ÷ 12 × $45)

Example: If you used 3 months → you pay for 3 months → refund the rest.

Exceptions

We reserve the right to deny refunds in cases of abuse, fraud, or excessive usage.
